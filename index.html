Your Parts Map

1.Click + New Client. Enter the client's name, your practitioner password, and a client password.
2.You'll get a unique URL for that client. Share the URL and their password with them — that's all they need to access their map.
3.Your password opens the practitioner view: full clinical notes, session log, and export tools. Their password opens the client view: their personal journey, what shifted, and what their parts asked for.
4.Use Bob (the chat panel) to build and update the map. Paste a session transcript, jot notes after a session, or just tell him what came up — he'll add parts, update the map, and write the log.
5.Parts live on the map spatially — Zone 1 (bottom) holds burdened parts and exiles, Zone 2 (middle) is the present and Self, Zone 3 (top) is for guides and anything returned to source. Parts move between zones as the work progresses.
